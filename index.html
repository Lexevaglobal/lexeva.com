Do you limit the number of schools?申请全案是否限制学校数量？

No. For the full application package, we do not set a school-number cap. Once the core application narrative is established, we adapt the materials to each target programme with school-specific research and positioning.不限制。对于申请全案客户，我们不会按学校数量设置上限。核心主线确定后，我们会根据每个目标项目的课程、学院特色和申请要求继续做针对性适配。

Do you guarantee admission?是否保证录取？

No. Admission depends on academic background, GPA, recommendation letters, writing samples, competition and university discretion. Our role is to maximise the quality, coherence and persuasiveness of the application materials.不保证，也不应承诺保证。录取结果受本科背景、GPA、推荐信、writing sample、竞争年份和学校独立判断影响。我们的工作是尽可能提升材料质量、逻辑完整性和说服力。

How are you different from large agencies?和大型机构有什么不同？

LEXEVA operates as a boutique advisory studio. We focus on research-driven writing, individual strategy and legal narrative construction rather than standardised document production.LEXEVA更接近精品顾问工作室，重点在研究型写作、个案策略和法律叙事搭建，而不是标准化批量生产。
